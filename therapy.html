Inspirational Therapy

OK.JOHN INSPIRATION THERAPY CORP (Non-profit)

Healing Through Faith, Prayer and Inspiration


OK.John offers Inspirational Therapy grounded in Christian faith and the power of prayer. As a gospel artist, inspirational speaker and author, his mission is to support individuals affected by drug abuse, addictions, violence and emotional struggles. Through spiritual guidance, prayer and biblical teachings, we help restore hope and rebuild lives.

Our Mission


We believe that true healing comes from God. Our goal is to help individuals reclaim:

  • Inner peace and mental alertness
  • Spiritual strength and clarity
  • Confidence and self-worth
  • A renewed sense of purpose in Christ

What We Offer


  • One-on-one inspirational and faith-based support
  • Prayer sessions and biblical encouragement
  • Youth mentorship and preventive counseling
  • Spiritual coaching for families and individuals
  • Community outreach and awareness programs

Why Inspirational Therapy?


Because prayer transforms. The Word heals. And through God’s grace, every life can rise again. Our approach combines faith, compassion, inspiration and practical guidance to help individuals walk toward healing and freedom.
