PRAYER PILL

Author : Ogaziri John Okereke

In this book, I, Ogaziri John Okereke, will walk you through the importance and power of prayers. I am a gospel artist, senior pastor of the prayer station Online Global Prayer Network. I currently work as a prayer therapist and youth care mentor in Saskatoon Sk. Canada. The purpose of writing this book is to help readers truly understand the significance of prayers and encourage everyone to pray regardless of being a believer or non-believer. I want you to connect with the Lord and experience quality living through prayer like never before. In this book, I will unravel everything that you need to know about praying, along with the consequences of prayerlessness. I will guide you through The four pillars that I believe can help you not only understand the path of righteousness but also set on it to experience a better and more content life in unthinkable ways. Through this book, I will help my readers use the power of prayers to simplify their lives and ensure that their Lord is happy with how they live and lead their lives. Prayer is the best way to bring light to darkness and move forward with life despite the challenges with hope, courage, and contentment. This book will show you how prayer is the best medicine to enjoying quality living—in this world and in the world hereafter.

MR. PANDEMIC’S Confessions

Author : Ogaziri John Okereke

The COVID-19 pandemic changes the world; it changes how many of us perceive ourselves, how we manage our relationships and how we build our thought processes. It brought many sorrows, afflictions, turbulences, and anxieties to many of the houses across the globe. But at the same time, it allowed us to see ourselves more clearly and analyse ourselves in the best possible way. Where many have lost relatives, friends and colleagues, we have also built some relations that carry the potential to last till life ends. The confessions we made this year are far bigger than we might have made in our lives.
